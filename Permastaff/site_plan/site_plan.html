Main Purpose

I know a few poeple in Mesa, Arizona who own a human resource company called Permastaff. They had a website created in 2007, but it hasn't been updated since and does look a little dated. The website is simple and basically just provides info about the company and the different benefits and employer would receive by outsourcing with them.
My goal with this website is to modernize the website a bit and give employers a better way to learn about what exactly Permastaff can do for them and how they can go about creating a partnership. There will be details human resources, payrolls, employee benefits, risk management, recruitment, and more.
