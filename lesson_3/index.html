Site Overview

A chamber of commerce has many different functions. It's main purpose is to serve as a support system for local business in the area it is established. A chamber of commerce is a network for for these business owners to collaborate and help, as well as to advocate for one another. They may also work together for the benefit of the community.

The purpose of this webstie is to serve as a hub for businesses and people in Grand Canyon Village, Arizona that wish to be apart of this network.
