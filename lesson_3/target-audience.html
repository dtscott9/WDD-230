Target Audience

I suspect that the main target audicence of this website would be someone who has recently started a business, or someone who is looking into starting a business. One factor that could help them to find success would be to network with other business owners in the area and exchange ideas and advice.

Another potential audience fot this website could be employees of businesses that have a membership in the chamber of congress. They may just want to be in the loop of events and programs involving the business that are in.

Potential Scenarios

Some potential scenarios in which a person would visit this website might include:

  • A recent business owner is looking to apply to become apart of the Grand Canyon Commerce.
  • A business owner who already holds a membership, but is checking the website to see if any meetings or events will be held in the near future
  • A busniess owner browsing through the website to look for opportunities to network with other business owners.
  • An employee of a company checking to see if their business is registered with Grand Canyon Commerce.
  • A new member of the chamber of commerce who is checking the commerce center is.
